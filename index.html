About Me

Although 5Ks ENV opened for business in 2020, I have been providing environmental and agribusiness consulting services to clients in Hawaii and the Pacific since 1986. My professional and advanced training in biology/marine sciences and environmental / occupational health has enabled me to recognize, appraise, and effectively resolve numerous environmental challenges. My clients project goals and objectives are met due to: a knowledge of local conditions; a strong relationships with regulators, sub-consultants, and community stakeholders; and an uncompromising commitment to client service.
